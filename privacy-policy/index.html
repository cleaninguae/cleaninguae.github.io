Privacy Policy for Cleaning Company UAE

At Cleaning Company UAE, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website (cleaninguae.github.io), use our cleaning services, or interact with us in the UAE. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Last Updated: May 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and address provided when you book cleaning services, submit forms (e.g., custom request forms), or contact us via WhatsApp or email.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, device type, and website usage data collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Service-Related Information: Details about your cleaning requests, such as property type, service preferences, and appointment details.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • Forms: Information you submit via our website forms (e.g., booking or custom request forms).
  • Communications: Data provided when you contact us via email, phone, or WhatsApp.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website performance. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Third-Party Tools: Analytics services like Google Analytics may collect anonymized data about your website interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and schedule cleaning services across the UAE, including sofa cleaning, villa cleaning, and pest control.
  • Communicate with you about bookings, service updates, or promotions.
  • Improve our website and services through analytics and user feedback.
  • Comply with legal obligations under UAE laws.
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent), such as special offers on cleaning services in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, or Sharjah.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (e.g., Formspree for form submissions) that assist with booking, payment processing, or analytics, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by UAE law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred with prior notice.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and improve user experience.
  • Analyze website traffic and performance.
  • Deliver personalized content or ads (if applicable).

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality. For more details, contact us at kalamansari1996@gmail.com.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encryption for form submissions and secure storage. However, no online system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

As a user in the UAE, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request information about how your data is used.
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority in the UAE.

To exercise these rights, contact us at kalamansari1996@gmail.com or via WhatsApp.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., social media or payment processors). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing information.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have such data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or UAE regulations. We will notify you via email or a website notice before significant changes take effect. Please review this page periodically.
